ABOUT

Outpost 31 is an independent studio that makes sound and music for games, screen and creative projects, covering the full production process from recording and editing through to mixing, mastering and broadcast.

From music cues and original soundtracks through to special effects, atmosphere and final polish.

It is run by Julian Costa, with more than 20 years of experience across production sound, post-production, broadcast, music and teaching. Outpost 31 is where that experience meets small games, practical sound work and the occasional strange object that needs recording.

Outpost 31 is a working production studio space in Mt Lawley, Western Australia, on Whadjuk Noongar Country.
