PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy applies to software, games, apps, websites, and related services published by Outpost 31 Productions.

Information Collection and Use

Outpost 31 Productions does not directly collect personal information through its software unless a service clearly states otherwise.

Some software may use third-party services, including Google Play Services, Google AdMob, Google Analytics, or similar providers. These services may collect limited technical information such as device information, IP address, crash data, app usage data, and advertising identifiers, in line with their own privacy policies.

Where advertising is shown, third-party advertising providers may use information to measure advertising performance and, where permitted, provide more relevant advertising.

Third-Party Services

Third-party services used by Outpost 31 Productions software may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob

These providers have their own privacy policies. Outpost 31 Productions does not control how third parties collect, store, or use information.

Log Data

In the event of an app error, third-party services may collect technical data known as log data. This may include device type, operating system version, app configuration, IP address, date and time of use, and diagnostic information.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Outpost 31 Productions software does not generally use cookies directly. However, third-party services used within an app or website may use cookies, SDKs, advertising identifiers, or similar technologies to provide, improve, measure, or support their services.

Children’s Privacy

Outpost 31 Productions does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Where a product is intended for children or families, data handling will follow the relevant store and platform requirements.

Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect information handled through Outpost 31 Productions services. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Links to Other Sites

Our software and websites may link to third-party sites or services. Outpost 31 Productions is not responsible for the content, privacy practices, or policies of those external services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The current version will always be published on this page.
